# **Nykaa Product Performance Analysis**  
### *Machine Learning & Customer Analytics Project*

---

## **Overview**
This project analyzes beauty product data from Nykaa to evaluate product performance and predict product success using machine learning techniques.  

The objective is to understand how customer engagement, ratings, and product attributes influence success in online beauty retail.

---

## **Tools & Technologies**
**Octoparse • Excel • Python**  
**Decision Tree • Random Forest • Logistic Regression**

---

## **Dataset**
- Scraped from Nykaa using Octoparse  
- ~9,800 products  
- Includes product details, pricing, ratings, reviews, and attributes  

---

## **Methodology**
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)  
- Feature engineering and encoding  

**Binary classification of product success:**
- Rating ≥ 4  
- Review count above median  

**Model building:**
- Decision Tree  
- Random Forest  
- Logistic Regression  

---

## **Key Results**
- Random Forest achieved the best overall performance  
- Decision Tree performed strongly  
- Logistic Regression performed poorly due to nonlinear relationships  

---

## **Key Insights**
- Customer engagement (review count) is the strongest predictor of product success  
- Higher-rated products with more reviews are more likely to succeed  
- Shade mismatch is a major source of customer dissatisfaction in beauty products  
- Product attributes like price and discounts have less influence compared to engagement  

---

## **Business Recommendation**
A key outcome of this analysis is the need to address shade mismatch, which frequently appears in negative customer feedback.  

**Recommendation:**  
Nykaa can implement an **AI-based shade matching system** that uses customer inputs (skin tone, previous purchases, or image-based analysis) to recommend accurate product shades.  

**Expected Impact:**
- Reduced product returns and dissatisfaction  
- Improved customer trust and experience  
- Higher conversion rates and product performance
